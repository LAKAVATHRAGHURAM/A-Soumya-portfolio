Short Bio

Soumya A is a Sr. Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Christu Jyothi Institute of Technology & Science, Hyderabad, India. She holds a B.Tech. degree in Information Technology and an M.Tech. degree from Jawaharlal Technological University, Hyderabad. With over 13 years of teaching experience, her expertise spans computer vision, machine learning, and LiDAR-based sensors, focusing on autonomous driving applications and advanced object detection techniques. Soumya has made significant contributions to deep learning and computer vision through research, publications, and academic mentorship.
